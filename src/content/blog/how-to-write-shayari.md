Writing is one of the purest forms of therapy. Whether you're feeling heartbreak, joy, or solitude, putting pen to paper (or thumb to screen) can help you process your inner world...

## Start with a Single Emotion
Don't try to write an epic. Focus on one specific feeling—the smell of rain, the silence of a midnight room, or the sting of a goodbye.

## Use Simple Metaphors
You don't need complex words. The best shayari often uses everyday objects to represent deep concepts.

## Find Your Rhythm
While free verse is popular, having a subtle rhythm or a soft rhyme can make your lines more memorable.
